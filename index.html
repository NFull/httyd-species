How To Train Your Dragon Species

This page provides an overview and brief description of the main characters and their dragons from the movie series "How To Train Your Dragon"!

How To Train Your Dragon

How To Train Your Dragon is a movie series that began with it's first movie release on March 21, 2010. The first movie was directed by both Chris Sanders and Dean Deblois, while the following movies were solely directed by Dean Dublois. The series began as an animated series under DreamWorks. There were a total of three movies that followed the main storyline, as well as several other spin-offs and shows. A live action adaptation of the movie was released on June 13, 2025, with a confirmation of a continuation of the series in live action format.

Main Characters and their Dragons

Hiccup and Toothless

  • Hiccup is the main protagonist of How To Train Your Dragon and is the son of the chief of his tribe.
  • This is his dragon, Toothless, who is a Night Fury. Night Fury's are known for their stealth and are generally a rare species of dragon.

Astrid and Stormfly

  • Astrid is the female lead of How To Train Your Dragon who is known for her strong will and battling ability.
  • This is her dragon, StormFly, who is a Deadly Nadder. They have razor sharp blades on their tails that they can use as projectiles.

Snotlout and Hookfang

  • Snotlout is a side character that was first introduced as a love rival for Astrid.
  • This is his dragon, HookFang, who is a Monstrous Nightmare. They can set themselves on fire and often have hot tempers.

Fishlegs and Meatlug

  • Fishlegs is a side character known for his nerdy personality and in-depth knowledge of dragons.
  • This is his dragon, Meatlug, who is a Gronckle. They are known for their heavyset bodies and sturdy round tails.

Ruffnut, Tuffnut, and Barf, and Belch

  • Ruffnut and Tuffnut are twins and side characters known for their funny and aloof personalities
  • This is their dragon, Barf and Belch, who is a Hideous Zippleback. One head spews poison and the other ignites it, causing a dangerous explosion.

Other Species

While How To Train Your Dragon mainly focuses on the main characters and their dragons, there are several other canonical species of dragons that are referenced in the movie and related How To Train Your Dragon content!
