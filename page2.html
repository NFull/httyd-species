This page provides an overview and brief descriptions of several dragon species

that weren't part of the main cast in the movie series "How To Train Your Dragon"!

Top 5 Fan-Favorite Dragon Species

There are more than a hundred canonical dragon species in the movie series "How To Train Your Dragon".

Due to this fact, this page will be limited to the top 5 fan-favorite dragon species and their riders (as applicable)

that are not part of the main cast. But don't worry! I will add a link to a page that includes

all the species of dragons towards the bottom of the page, if you wish to see them all!

With that said, the dragons that I will be sharing on this page are as follows:

  1. Cloudjumper
  2. Thornado
  3. The Skrill
  4. Light Fury
  5. Crimson Goregutter

1. Valka and Cloudjumper

  • Valka is Hiccup's mother, who was assumed to be dead after a dragon attack when he was a baby. They were reunited in HTTYD2 and she moved back to their tribe.
  • Cloudjumper is her dragon, who is a Stormcutter. These dragons have owl-like attributes along with great flexibility and sharp senses.

Stoick and Thornado

  • Stoick is Hiccup's father and chief of their tribe. He is warrior with great skill in both strategy and combat.
  • Thornado was his dragon in the Dragons: Rider of Berk series. It is a Thunderdrum, which are known for their ability to emit a concussive sound that kills.
